About Us

Has S. Jawandha, Esq. - Experienced Trial Attorney

Has S. Jawandha is an experienced, knowledgeable, and zealous advocate for the people. He has experience in a broad array of practice areas, including, personal/catastrophic injury, wrongful death, premises liability, products liability, and motor vehicle and trucking accidents. He takes immense pride in providing his legal representation and knowledge to those that have been severely and catastrophically injured due to the negligence or intentional conduct of others. Has has experience in handling various civil litigation disputes from inception till resolution, including trial and alternative dispute resolution.
Prior to founding his own personal injury and civil litigation law firm, Has honed his knowledge and skill set by practicing as an insurance defense trial attorney working in-house for one of the largest insurance corporations in the country. Once being in the shoes of his now adversaries, Has gained first-hand knowledge in the methods and tactics of defense counsel and the insurance claims industry, which he now effectively uses to anticipate his opponents’ strategies and actions – ultimately benefiting his clients in the litigation process. Prior to gaining experience in the processes and procedures of insurance defense, Has worked as a trial attorney representing individuals who were victims of violent assaults and batteries by employees of corporate entities, as well as those that were catastrophically injured due to corporate negligence.
Before Has began practicing as an attorney, he earned his Juris Doctorate from the University of San Francisco School of Law, where he excelled in academics, as well as extracurricular involvement. Has successfully completed USF Law’s Business Law Certificate program (emphasis in corporate, commercial, and business law), which he graduated from with honors. Additionally, Has was a member of USF Law’s Saul Lefkowitz Moot Court competition team, he served as President of the Real Estate Law Society, and was an active member of the Business Law Society and the Environmental Law Society. Preceding law school, Has graduated from the University of California, Irvine, where he earned his Bachelor of Arts in Criminology, Law and Society.
Has’s willingness to represent the individual and zealously fight for the injured arises from his familial, cultural, and athletic background. He was born and raised in a hardworking Punjabi family and he is a Sikh. Having grown up as a wrestler and holder of a third-degree black belt in Kajukenbo, as well as earning the title of Sifu (Black Belt Instructor), the values of mental and physical toughness, sharpness, discipline, and the courage and bravery to stand up and defend those in need were instilled in him from very early on in his life. Inherently, Has takes pride in his calling to stand up for those with righteous causes against corporate giants and their insurance companies.
When Has is not zealously advocating on behalf of his clients, he enjoys weightlifting, playing and watching all sports, helping his family at their East Bay pizza restaurant – where he was pretty much born and raised, and traveling with his wife.
