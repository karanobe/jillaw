Personal Injury

At Jawandha Injury Law we take pride in representing individuals that have suffered physical and emotional harm due to the negligence or intentional conduct of another. Jawandha Injury Law’s attorney is an expert in the field of personal injury matters in litigation. Each client and their respective case are treated with the utmost compassion and zealous representation.

Car & Bicycle Accidents

If you have been involved in a car or bicycle accident, or been struck as a pedestrian, then Jawandha Injury Law can help you recover from the harms and losses that you have suffered.

Premises Liability

If you have suffered injuries on another’s property due to the negligence or intentional conduct of another, then Jawandha Injury Law can help you in recovering and receiving the compensation that you are entitled to.

Dog-bite Incidents

Dog bite cases involve legal claims arising from injuries sustained due to dog attacks. In these cases, victims may seek compensation for medical expenses, emotional trauma, lost wages, and other damages. Jawandha Injury Law can help victims navigate the complexities of these cases, ensuring they receive the justice and compensation they deserve.

Wrongful Death

Wrongful death cases arise when an individual dies due to the negligence or misconduct of another party. These cases seek to hold responsible parties accountable and provide compensation to the deceased's family for loss of income, funeral expenses, and emotional suffering. Common causes include car accidents, medical malpractice, and workplace incidents. Jawandha Injury Law can guide families through the legal process, helping them pursue justice and closure during a difficult time.

Medical Malpractice

Medical malpractice cases involve claims against healthcare professionals who fail to provide the standard of care, resulting in harm to patients. This can include misdiagnosis, surgical errors, medication mistakes, or inadequate treatment. Victims may seek compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, and ongoing care needs. Jawandha Injury Law can help navigate the complexities of medical malpractice law, ensuring that victims receive the justice and compensation they deserve for their injuries.

Civil Litigation

Civil litigation cases involve legal disputes between individuals or entities seeking compensation or resolution outside of criminal law. In the context of personal injury, this can include a wide range of issues, such as negligence, contract disputes, and property damage. Jawandha Injury Law represents clients in these cases, advocating for their rights and working to secure favorable outcomes through negotiation or trial. With a focus on protecting clients' interests, Jawandha Injury Law’s experienced attorneys can navigate the complexities of civil litigation to achieve justice and compensation for the injured.

Frequently asked
questions

  • When do I have a personal injury case?
    You or a loved one may have a personal injury case if you or they have been injured due to someone else's negligence or intentional actions, and the injury has caused physical, emotional, or financial harm. To determine if you or your loved one has a valid case, it's important to evaluate factors like liability, the extent of your injury, and whether you can prove the other party's fault. Contact Jawandha Injury Law as soon as possible to to discuss with an experienced attorney whether you or your loved one has a personal injury case.
  • What should I do if I am involved in a personal injury incident?
    If you're involved in a personal injury incident, the first thing you should do is seek medical attention, even if you don't feel seriously injured. Then, make sure to document the scene, gather contact information from witnesses, and report the incident to the appropriate authorities, while avoiding admitting fault or discussing details until you can consult with an experience personal injury lawyer at Jawandha Injury Law.
  • How much time do I have to file a claim or lawsuit?
    In California, the statute of limitations for filing a personal injury claim is generally two years from the date of the injury. However, certain circumstances may shorten or extend this timeframe, such as when bringing a claim for personal injuries against a government entity. Therefore, it's important to consult with an experienced attorney as soon as possible to ensure your claim is filed on time. Contact Jawandha Injury Law right away to discuss when your claim or lawsuit may need to be filed.
